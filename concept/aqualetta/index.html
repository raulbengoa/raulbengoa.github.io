Aqualetta

Aqualetta is a design project completed within the University of A Coruña that aimed to create an innovative product appealing to the public, that could fit into an existing market gap, and that was viable as a commercialized product.

Our design team was given total liberty, with no artificial restrictions so we could engage our full creativity into the design process. The resulting project needed to demonstrate engaging aesthetics, market feasibility and sound engineering practices.

The outcome of the project is Aqualetta, the first hybrid aquatic foldable reclining chair that offers a pedal propulsion system. This novel system affords Aqualetta both land mobility and water navigation capabilities.

The final design takes into account the resistance, material, costs, ease of manufacturing, weight, transportation, etc. for a complete production package showcase. It also includes:

  • Market analysis reports

  • Full production plan, including:

    • Factory design with machinery and worker costs
    • Flow chart of the production process
    • Sourcing of providers for all the materials involved
