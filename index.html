Product & Graphic Design

Raul Bengoa Design accumulates years of design experience, with multiple successful products on the market that have consistently received excellent reviews by both customers and end users.

Product design is concerned with any product that a customer wants to take from specifications to production runs, offering excellent designs that are built on a solid engineering base that makes them ready to withstand the rigors of real-world, everyday usage. The services offered in this area by Raul Bengoa Design include (but are not limited to):

  • Specification gathering and discussion.

  • Market fit analysis.

  • High-level design.

  • Fast prototyping.

  • Engineering analysis.

  • Production costs analysis.

In parallel to the actual design of products, Raul Bengoa Design also offers high-quality graphic design services to create a full package that a customer can use to take a product to market with minimal effort. Graphic design services include:

  • Corporate branding items, such as logos, color schemas, etc.

  • Product differentiation between different product lines and within a single product line.

  • Sales materials, such as brochures, flyers, slide decks, etc.

  • Marketing collateral, such as specification sheets, posters, stickers, etc.

  • Translation and localization to multiple languages and markets, for worlwide distribution.

3D Modeling and Rendering

Raul Bengoa Design uses the latest tools and technologies to create immersive 3D modeling to fulfill any customer demand. 3D modeling-heavy projects present their own set of challenges, and years of experience ensure that Raul Bengoa Design will deliver results meeting or surpassing customers' expectations.

Services provided in this area include:

  • Base 3D modeling from informal specifications and/or freehand sketches.

  • Quick prototyping and iteration of models, with very fast turnaround.

  • Exploration of different approaches with validation by the customer at every point of the process.

  • Usage of a wide array of modeling programs, suited for every need that may arise. 3D Studio for fast, highly editable concept design, Solidworks for engineering high precision parts, Rhinoceros for NURBS surfaces...

  • The best modeling techniques are selected for each individual project, from regular mesh manipulation techniques, to NURBs modeling, to solid geometry boolean manipulation.

  • Rendering that adapts to the intended usage: any rendering technique within the full palette, from technical, engineering-friendly exact renderings (wireframe or flat shaded), to fully artistic renderings with realistic lighting and film-like postprocessing effects.

  • After-rendering postprocessing and editing.

Conceptual Design

Conceptual design projects allow Raul Bengoa Design to fully explore the space of creativity for any given problem. There are no boundaries or limits, so truly innovative solutions arise throughout the interaction with customers during the design process.

This type of projects is usually more open-ended than specific product design tasks, so services provided in this area include, but are not limited to:

  • Consultation with customers at early stages of conception

  • Face-to-face brainstorming sessions

  • Assesment of feasibility from the engineering and production points of view

  • Time to market considerations

All of which ensure that the early conceptual explorations provided by Raul Bengoa Design cast a solid foundation for future product design in the given space.
